Name : Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and one of the most prominent figures in world cricket.

    Early Life

  1. Date of Birth : Novemeber 5,1988
  2. Place of Birth : /Delhi , India
  3. Family: He was born to prem Kohli and Saroj Kohli . He has an elder brother , vikas and an elder sister , Bhawna.

    Cricket career

  1. Domestic Cricket
    • Played for Delhi in domestic cricket.
    • Led the Indian Under 19 team to victory in the 2008 ICC Under-19 Cricket World Cup.
  2. International Debut:
    • ODI Debut: August 18 , 2008 , against Sri Lanka.
    • Test Debut: June 20, 2011 , against West Indies.
    • T20I Debut: June 12, 2010, against Zimbabwe.
  3. Playing style:
    • Right-handed batsman known for his aggressive batting style and consistency.
    • Occasional right-arm medium pace bowler.
  4. Captaincy:
    • Became the captain of the Indian Test team in 2014.
    • Took over as captain of the limited-overs formats in 2017.
    • Stepped down as T20I captain in 2021 and as ODI captain in 2022.
    • Stepped down from Test captaincy in January 2022.
  5. Records and Achievements
    • Centuries: One of the highest century-makers in international cricket, with numerous ceturies across all formats.
    • Awards: Has won several ICC awards including the ICC ODI Player of the Year and the Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy for ICC Cricketer of the Year.
    • Fastest to 8000,9000,10000,11000 and 12000 runs in ODIs.
    • Only player to average over 50 in all formats of the game.
    • Leading run-scorer in T20 Internationas.
